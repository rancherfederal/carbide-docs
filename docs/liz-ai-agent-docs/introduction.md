# Introduction

## What is Liz?

Liz is a context-aware AI agent integrated into your Rancher Government Carbide environment. Unlike general-purpose AI, Liz is designed to understand the live state of your clusters, namespaces, and workloads.

Available as an extension, Liz resides within your workspace to provide real-time guidance where you need it. Whether you are investigating a failing deployment or trying to understand a new Kubernetes concept, Liz uses the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to bridge the gap between your operational data and actionable insights.

Whether you need a Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) opinion or the help of a Rancher expert, Liz can provide you insight to help you make the right decision, faster. The power of Liz lies in its integration with the Rancher UI and its ability to adapt to enterprise security requirements.
